Meet Ramya

Hello! I’m Ramya – a 21-year-old Indian currently in my 4th year of university at Syracuse University. I was born in India and raised in Nigeria and Dubai – so I am a multicultural kid. No matter where I travelled, food was something that always interested me. Now that I have lived away from my parents for more than three years, I have experimented with a variety of dishes that appeal to my tastebuds. But nothing beats my mom’s homecooked meal! Now that I have spent a good amount of time in the kitchen and learned so much more about Indian food and the spices that it contains, I am ready to share it with the world!

Everyday there are new discoveries that a person can learn and implement in their life. This world is huge! My goal is to share and educate the benefits of these Indian spices and how you can incorporate them into your everyday cooking.

Please feel free to send your recreations with feedback or just to say hi!
